A passion for solving all skincare concerns. Extensive, in-depth knowledge of brands, ingredients and the latest products and procedures. As an Aesthetician and founder of Christina’s Aesthetics, I take each client’s unique skincare routines, add my advice and services and create lifelong partnerships that achieve and maintain clear, healthy skin.

My journey into skincare started after I graduated as a Licensed Aesthetician from The Paul Mitchell Academy in Orange County, CA in 2004. In February 2007, I began working at Sephora and received specialized training in classic, cosmeceutical, and natural/botanical skincare brands. While at Sephora, I became an Education Lead, worked alongside various brand representatives and met Dr. Nicholas Perricone, whose philosophy of skincare through nutrition is a belief that I pass on to my clients to this day. After six years at Sephora, I used my education skills and built a returning clientele at several specialty and luxury retail stores, including Ulta and Space NK.

At Christina’s Aesthetics, I specialize in facial massages, customized facials and chemical peels. I have the gift of reading each client accordingly, which allows me to build a rapport with all walks of life. You’re the quiet type? You’ll have a calm, sensory-related experience. More of a social butterfly? I break down and answer all your questions, such as the latest trends and what really works. The ever-evolving world of skincare prompts my continuing education of staying updated with the latest “it” ingredients, along with promoting gold standards, including retinols and SPFs.
Let me help make you feel comfortable in your own skin. Book an appointment today and let’s make your skin its best version for 2021.
